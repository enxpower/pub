品牌故事Brand Story

华夏文明，和而不同Heritage · Identity · Vision

品牌源起 · 文化内核 · 未来愿景Origin · Cultural Core · Future Vision

华夏和生于这样一种信念：中华文明所承载的静默之美，是世界尚未完全发现的奢华。不是节庆的红漆，不是出口陶瓷的镀金繁华——而是文人的砚台、僧侣的茶碗、笔触之间的留白。我们存在的意义，是将这份深度转化为器物、仪式与体验，令其在北京与巴黎同样从容，在东京与伦敦同样自然。

Huaxiahe is born from the conviction that Chinese civilization carries a quiet luxury the modern world has yet to fully discover. Not the red lacquer of festivals, nor the gilded excess of export ceramics — but the scholar's stone, the monk's tea bowl, the breath between brushstrokes. We exist to translate that depth into objects, rituals, and experiences that belong equally in Beijing and Paris, Tokyo and London.

华
繁盛之意Huá — Splendor

「华」言繁盛，道出一个文明的花开——艺术、哲学、器物工艺，经五千年淬炼。品牌从中汲取传承，并以之为标尺自律。

The character 华 speaks of florescence — civilization in full bloom. It carries the weight of millennia: the arts, the sciences, the philosophy refined across five thousand years. It is the inheritance we draw from, and the standard we hold ourselves to.

夏
华夏故土Xià — The Great Land

「夏」指向上古，是中华第一王朝，文明的起源神话。它将华夏和锚定于地方与时间的连续性中——最恒久的事物，皆生长于被一代代耕耘的土地。

夏 names the ancient heartland — China's first dynasty, the origin myth of a civilization. It grounds us in place and continuity, reminding us that the most enduring things grow slowly, from soil that has been worked for generations.

和
和谐之道Hé — Harmony

「和」是品牌中最核心的字，也是中国哲学中最重要的字。不只是平静——而是对立间的动态平衡：冷与热、虚与实、古与新。我们所造的每一件物，都在追求这种和。

和 is the most essential character in the brand, and in Chinese philosophy itself. It is the dynamic balance between opposites — hot and cold, full and empty, old and new. Every product we make aspires to this harmony.

标志系统Logo System

标志与变体Primary Mark · Variations

主标志 · 变体 · 使用规范Primary · Variants · Usage Rules

华夏和标志是字母组合曼陀罗：华夏和三字首字母（H·X·H）交织于传统花瓣框架之中，中心置一香炉。图形同时是印章、徽章与窗棂——指向中华物质文化的三大支柱。渐变从暖白过渡至深铜，象征原料到精工的旅程。

The Huaxiahe logo is a monogram-mandala: the initials H·X·H interlaced within a classical petal frame, with an incense burner at its center. The form is simultaneously a seal, a crest, and a window lattice. The gradient transitions from warm ivory to deep bronze, reflecting the journey from raw material to refined craft.

最小保护区域Minimum Clear Space

标志四周须保留等于标志总高度六分之一的空白区域，此区域内不得出现任何文字、图形或页边。最小复制尺寸：24px / 8mm。小于此尺寸请改用独立图标版。

The logo must always be surrounded by clear space equal to one-sixth of its total height on all sides. Minimum reproduction: 24px / 8mm. Below this threshold, use the icon mark only.

色彩体系Color System

主色调与辅助色Primary Palette · Secondary Tones

主色 · 辅色 · 用色规范Primary · Secondary · Usage Rules

色板直接提取自标志渐变：从窑白象牙，经暖亚麻，到自然金，最终沉淀为老漆器般的深琥珀。色彩使用保持高度克制——视线应首先落于留白。

The palette is drawn from the logo's own gradient: from kiln-white ivory through warm linen, natural gold, and settling into the deep amber of aged lacquerware. Colors are used with great restraint — the eye should always rest on negative space first.

浅色版式Light Compositions

以暖白（#FAF8F5）或麻白为画布。正文使用石灰色。深琥珀用于标题。品牌金用于点缀与规则线。留白是第一设计法则。

Use Warm White or Linen as canvas. Body in Stone. Amber Deep for headings. Brand Gold for accents. Generosity with negative space is the first rule.

深色版式Dark Compositions

以墨黑（#1A1612）为底。象牙白与暖亚麻建立文字层次。品牌金用于点缀元素。避免纯黑底——始终使用带暖意的墨色调。

Use Ink Black as base. Ivory and Warm Linen for type hierarchy. Brand Gold for accents. Always use the warm ink tone — avoid pure black backgrounds.

渐变用法Gradient Use

主标志渐变（象牙→品牌金→琥珀）仅可用于大标题展示时刻、包装丝带与品牌印章。不得用于正文或功能性界面元素。

The primary logo gradient may be applied to typographic display moments, packaging ribbons, and brand seals only. Never apply to body text or functional UI elements.

用色克制Color Restraint

任何单一版式不得同时使用超过三种色板色值。留白是一种色彩。一行琥珀标题在白页上传递的奢华感，胜过满版的金色。

No single layout should use more than three palette values simultaneously. Silence is a color. One amber heading on a white page communicates more luxury than a page covered in gold.

字体系统Typography System

字体规范与层级Type Scale · Roles · Pairing

字阶 · 字重 · 中英文配对Scale · Weight · Bilingual Pairing

字体形象由两个衬线传统并置构成。Cormorant Garamond 带来欧洲经典书编的优雅——其纤细笔画与中国细线版画的精致相呼应。Noto Serif SC 将中文锚定于宋刻传统：结构端正、从容舒展、各尺寸均高度可读。两种书写传统是并置，而非翻译关系。

The typographic identity pairs two serif traditions. Cormorant Garamond brings classical European editorial grace — its delicate hairlines mirror the refinement of Chinese fine-line engraving. Noto Serif SC grounds Chinese type in the Song tradition: structured, unhurried, deeply readable. Two traditions in dialogue — not translation.

视觉语言Graphic Language

图形元素与纹理系统Motifs · Texture · Pattern · Form

纹样 · 肌理 · 图案 · 形态Motif · Texture · Pattern · Form

品牌图形词汇建立于五个来源：宋代窗棂的节律几何、水墨的呼吸感、篆刻的精度、竹的有机轮廓，以及宣纸的安静表面。每一个装饰决定都可追溯至其中之一。

The brand's graphic vocabulary is built from five sources: Song dynasty window lattice geometry, the breath of ink wash painting, the precision of seal carving, the organic silhouette of bamboo, and the quiet surface of rice paper. Every decorative decision traces back to one of these roots.

摄影方向Photography Direction

光线·构图·情绪·材质Light · Composition · Mood · Material

用光规范 · 构图法则 · 场景类型Lighting · Composition · Scene Types

所有华夏和摄影遵循一个原则：画面应有如暂停。仅使用自然侧光或北向漫射窗光，不用闪光灯，不做高饱和修图。背景选用白石膏、原色亚麻、旧木或灰石。道具以质感取胜，而非颜色。

All Huaxiahe photography obeys a single principle: the image should feel like a pause. Natural side-light or diffuse north-facing window light only. No flash. No high-saturation edits. Backgrounds: white plaster, raw linen, aged wood, gray stone. Props are chosen for their texture, not their color.

◻产品主图Product Shot

主体产品Hero Product

单主体，大留白，45°柔和侧光。暖白或石灰底背景。阴影不硬切。略俯角拍摄以展示器物内腔。

Single subject, generous negative space. 45° soft side-light. Warm white or stone gray background. No hard shadow cuts. Slightly elevated angle to reveal vessel interiors.

◻生活方式Lifestyle

生活场景Lifestyle

手入镜。捕捉行为之前或之后，而非进行中。晨光透过障子门。茶水正在被倒，而非被握持。香烟上升，而非正被点燃。

Hands in frame. The moment before or after, never during. Morning light through shoji. Tea being poured, not held. Incense smoke ascending, not lit.

◻工艺细节Craft Detail

微距工艺Craft Detail

极近距拍摄纹理：木纹、釉面、纤维、刻痕。浅景深。展示器物背后的工时，此处是品牌溢价的视觉来源。

Ultra-close texture shots of grain, glaze, fiber, carving. Shallow depth of field. Shows the hours behind the object — this is where the brand premium is made visible.

◻氛围空间Ambient Scene

空间与氛围Space & Atmosphere

宽画幅的仪式空间——茶台、书房、窗边。器物在使用中，而非陈列中。房间在呼吸。无刻意对称；以中国古典静物画的不对称构图为参照。

Wide scenes of ritual spaces — tea table, study, window seat. Objects in use, not on display. The room breathes. Asymmetric placement following classical Chinese still-life composition.

包装体系Packaging System

器物包装与品牌物料Gift Box · Label · Card · Letterhead

礼盒 · 标签 · 名片 · 信笺 · 香道盒 · 封蜡Gift Box · Label · Business Card · Letterhead · Incense Box · Wax Seal

包装是品牌与用户最初的实体接触。每一个表面都以与内容物同等的克制来对待：非涂布天然纸，压凹或烫金印标志，极简文字。拆开的过程应当像开启一封来自信任之人的信函。

Packaging is the first physical encounter with the brand. Every surface is treated with the same discipline as the product inside: uncoated natural stock, debossed or foil-stamped marks, minimal text. The unboxing should feel like opening a letter from a trusted friend.

品牌语调Brand Voice

说话的方式The Way We Speak

语调 · 写作 · 原则 · 沟通Tone · Writing · Principles · Communication

华夏和的沟通方式，如同一位技艺精湛的主人倒茶：全神贯注，不急不躁，不加解释。选词只为承载其含义，而非展示自身。语调是亲密且有文化底气的——绝非学术腔，也绝非商业腔。

Huaxiahe communicates the way a skilled host pours tea: with full attention, without hurry, without explanation. Words are chosen for what they carry, not what they show off. The register is intimate and literate — never academic, never commercial.

01
从容不迫Unhurried

永不催促，永不宣告。让读者仿佛出于自身好奇而自然抵达品牌。

Never push, never announce. Let the reader arrive at the product as if by their own curiosity.

02
具体精准Concrete

命名材料、产地和工艺。「四川柏木」而非「天然木材」。精确赢得信任。

Name materials, origins, and processes. "Sichuan cypress" not "natural wood." Precision earns trust.

03
双语对等Bilingual Parity

中文与英文地位平等。两者均非对方的翻译——各自以母语读者的方式说话。

Chinese and English are equals. Neither is a translation of the other. Each speaks to its reader natively.

04
以少胜多Silence Earns Space

少即是多。一句美好的句子胜过五句功能性句子。毫不留情地删改。

Less copy is always more. One beautiful sentence outperforms five functional ones. Edit without mercy.
