合香珠，不是普通珠串Not Carved From a Single Material

合香工艺 · 佩戴之物Blended Incense, Made to Be Worn

合香珠，不是以单一木料制成的普通珠串。它将木、花、本草与天然芳香材料，依照气味、质地与节奏加以调和，经研磨、配伍、和泥、成珠与缓慢养藏，成为可以贴身佩戴的香气之物。

Blended incense beads are not carved from a single piece of wood. They bring aromatic woods, flowers, botanicals, and resins into one balanced composition — prepared, ground, blended, shaped, and slowly rested into an object designed to be worn close to the body.

佩戴Wearing

贴身之物Carried Close

触感 · 温度 · 时间Touch, Warmth, Time

一枚珠，握在掌心时微凉，贴身之后渐暖。它不以耀眼取胜，而在日复一日的佩戴中，留下属于人的气息与时间。

A bead, cool in the palm, warms slowly against the body. It does not announce itself — day by day, it takes on something of the person who wears it.
